Introduction

GloTitans is training, placement and IT firm with focus on quality and professionalism. It is founded with zeal to connect youth with corporate sector. It is the brain child of passionate corporate professionals who have served the industry for more than a decade. Glotitans provides soft skill training and job oriented training programmes to college students, graduates, technical grads and diploma holders to facilitate their entry into corporate sector with right perspective and prepared mind. Glotitans partners with corporate clients for their trained talent requirement through placement and training programmes. Software development, mobile applications and website development forms the key arm of Glotitans. Glotitans seeks to use its trained youth to deliver high end mobile apps and websites.
