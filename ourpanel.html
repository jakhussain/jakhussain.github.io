Founder & Director
Mohammad Shahid is the founder and driving force behind the GoTitans. He put behind the lucrative career in corporate to pursue his own entrepreneurial path.

He is an educationist at heart and constantly dreaming of connecting the gap between academia and industry though technological and innovative means. He aspires to take the professionalism and quality aspects of industry to colleges and universities. He is working in the industry for more than a decade.

He carries with him the valued experience of working across big corporate house Reliance Infrastructure to a founding member of Vema Solar Pvt Ltd in green technology. In his previous life he was responsible for business strategy, business development, analysis, financial management, taxation and accounts.

Our Team is committed to our values and pride ourselves on our impeccable ethics. At GloTitans, we share a keen sense of humor, love for what we do and a common concern for how we can create the most value for our each candidate and project. For us, each new project is an invaluable opportunity to forge lasting relationships.

The team has passion for our clients and candidates’ success and a pragmatic drive for action that starts each day with new vigour. As a team we endeavour to build a great culture of professionalism and commitment to our goal. We seek to translate the same to all our trainees across various programmes.
