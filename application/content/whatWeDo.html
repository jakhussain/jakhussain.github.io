Short Term Courses

Job Oriented Soft Skills: GloTitans organizes training on soft skills that help students to be 'day-one job ready'.. The training has a strong focus on soft skills including interview preparation and communication skill. computing skills and quality management system. The curriculum is designed considering the challenges industry faces at entry level employees from management & engineering trainees to technical workers.

Tally with GST: The candidate learns basic and advance accounting functions of tally with GST. The training also involves basic aspects of GST. On completing a financial accounting course, candidate can build a successful career as Accounts professional

Taxation: It is designed to update business small business owners and accounts students. The training is provided on GST and Income Tax. The learner is trained on concept, registration, exemptions, tax slabs, form filing and filing returns.

Financial Modeling & Advance Excel:The course is designed for practicing professionals, finance and accounts students. The candidate learns building financial model from the basics in spreadsheet. This course trains the candidate in advance functions of excel.

Budgeting, Planning & Management Reporting: The course is designed for practicing professionals, finance and accounts students. The candidate learns the budget preparation technique and process, planning aspects of the corporate and Management Information System.

Office Automation: This course offers training on essential office tools used in any organization and equip the learner to use them. The candidate learns the basic components of computers and terminology, understand data, information and file management, work efficiently using Word processor, Spreadsheet & Presentation, understand computer networks, and browse the internet, content search, email and collaborate with peers.

Data Science: The learners are trained in core mathematical foundation of analytics. The course includes R Programming, statistics & probability essentials. The trainers are experienced working corporate professionals.
