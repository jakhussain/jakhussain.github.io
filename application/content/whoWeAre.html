GloTitans is training, placement and IT firm with focus on quality and professionalism. It is founded with zeal to connect youth with corporate sector.

It is the brain child of passionate corporate professionals who have served the industry for more than a decade.

Glotitans provides soft skill training and job oriented training programmes to college students, graduates, technical grads and diploma holders to facilitate their entry into corporate sector with right perspective and prepared mind.

Glotitans partners with corporate clients for their trained talent requirement through placement and training programmes.

Software development, mobile applications and website development forms the key arm of Glotitans. Glotitans seeks to use its trained youth to deliver high end mobile apps and websites.

Our Vision:

To be the most trusted company with global outlook, bridging gap between academia & industry and promoting professionalism and quality.

Our Mission:

To deliver industry oriented skill & knowledge, quality manpower and latest technology in time bound manner with excellence.

Our Values:

  • Client first: Client need is the source of our motivation in all our action and thought.
  • Integrity: Transparency in action and sincerity of efforts.
  • Focus : Focus on delivering the best.
  • Passion : Positivity and craving to serve best.
